Who Are We?

Sweet Gloria is your one stop shop for all things Tea, Matcha, and Ice Cream. We pride ourselves on our photogenic ice-cream that refreshes your taste buds on a hot summer day. You'll absolutely love the creativity of flavors that we, at Sweet Gloria, offer.

Dec out our soft serve with your choice of beautiful toppings to make your Ice Cream #Instagram ready!

Our partner, Charles Feng, says "We consider every single ice cream as an art piece, that’s why we do the gold flakes."

We also have two monthly-changing soft serve flavors. You’ll find things like sweet lychee rose and yummy sea salt mascarpone.

You'll find us in Nolita, right near Prince Street Pizza if you decide you want to eat dinner like the happiest 10-year-old in the world.
